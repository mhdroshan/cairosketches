Who We Are

Cairo Sketches is a leading interior design and architectural firm

At Cairo Sketches, we transform spaces into captivating stories of elegance, functionality, and personality. Whether crafting sophisticated residential interiors, dynamic office spaces, or luxurious hospitality settings, our team of passionate designers and architects work meticulously to bring visions to life. With a commitment to innovative design solutions, premium materials, and impeccable attention to detail, we redefine living and working environments — ensuring every project is as unique as the people who inhabit it.

02
What we do?

Designing spaces that inspire and connect

Our mission is to create spaces that are not only designed but experienced — where every corner tells a story and every detail adds harmony to life. From tailored residential designs to dynamic commercial spaces, we turn ideas into reality. Our comprehensive services include space planning, 3D visualizations, custom furniture design, MEP integration, and landscaping. We believe in blending artistry with practicality to spark imagination and connection in every space we create.

Contact us today for your free consultation.
